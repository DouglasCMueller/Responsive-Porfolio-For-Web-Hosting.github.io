About Me

Douglas C. Mueller is a strategic, people-focused financial expert skilled at driving business growth and profitability within public and private sectors. With over 15 years of industry experience, including regulatory compliance, relationship building, policy and procedure creation, and employee management, he blends sharp financial acumen with an exceptional leadership ability to deliver impactful outcomes. Doug is currently a General Partner at Slainte Group LLC, where he directs business administration and management functions to support a variety of services, from venture and private equity to public investing. Managing a $1M portfolio comprised of venture investments, he leverages an in-depth knowledge of emerging finance techniques and best practices to source and assess viable opportunities. Collaborative by nature, he partners with accounting experts and attorneys to maintain tax and budgeting compliance with pertinent regulations. Previously, Doug worked cross-functionally as Managing Director & Head of Trading at Andor Capital LP to facilitate seamless daily operations. He guided strategic decision making and problem resolution for clients and internal executive committees. During his tenure, he managed proprietary trading of a $25M syndicate book, fostered industrious relationships with sales traders and research sales teams, and was a key member on the Soft Dollar and Brokerage committees. As Principal & Head of Technology Trading at Pequot Capital, Doug enabled timely, effective buy and sell securities orders and executed trading processes for a $20B fund, including the equities, derivatives, and foreign exchange of a technology portfolio. Regarded as a subject matter expert in relationship management, Doug excels at forging and sustaining positive relationships with individuals and groups at all levels. He effectively implements and communicates solutions that boost engagement among colleagues, supervisors, and clients, spurring goal-attaining results. At Andor Capital LP, he promoted a collaborative environment while coordinating global 24-hour trading desk functions with 12 traders, reaching $100M in annual sales commissions. He mentored junior traders, coaching on skills critical to meet stakeholder needs in a demanding and fast-paced environment. Doug holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Accounting & Finance from Fairfield University. Dedicated to continuous skills improvement, he is currently completing a full-stack web development course from the University of Connecticut School of Engineering. Residing in New Canaan, Connecticut with his wife and four children, Doug serves as a youth sports trainer, coach, and mentor devoted to equipping young athletes with the resources needed to be successful on the field, in the classroom, and in the workforce.
